### M&A Process

1. **Screening (4-6 weeks)**
   - Market mapping and target identification
   - Strategic fit evaluation
   - Preliminary financial analysis
   - Go/no-go decision

2. **Due Diligence (8-12 weeks)**
   - Financial DD: Revenue quality, profitability, cash flow
   - Legal DD: Corporate structure, IP, contracts, compliance
   - Technology DD: Architecture, code quality, security, scalability
   - Commercial DD: Market size, competitive landscape, customers
   - Operational DD: Leadership, employees, operations, quality

3. **Valuation & Negotiation (6-8 weeks)**
   - DCF valuation with 5-year projections
   - Comparable company analysis (comps)
   - Precedent transaction analysis
   - Financial modeling (base/bull/bear cases)
   - LOI and definitive agreement negotiation

4. **Integration Planning (4-6 weeks)**
   - Integration roadmap development
   - Workstream definition and resource allocation
   - Day 1 planning and readiness
   - Communication plan development

5. **Closing & Integration (12-18 months)**
   - Legal close and regulatory approvals
   - Day 1 execution
   - Integration execution across 9 workstreams
   - Synergy realization and value creation
