## üîç Competitive Landscape

### Similar Work

**1. Meta's RDMA for Distributed Training**
- **Similarity:** RDMA-based communication
- **Difference:** DWCP adds WAN optimization, multi-tier architecture
- **Advantage:** DWCP designed for internet-scale, not just datacenter

**2. OmniDMA (WAN RDMA)**
- **Similarity:** RDMA over WAN
- **Difference:** DWCP adds hierarchical encoding, adaptive consensus
- **Advantage:** DWCP is comprehensive framework, not just transport

**3. TT-Prune (Federated Learning)**
- **Similarity:** Model pruning for communication reduction
- **Difference:** DWCP integrates with multi-stream transport, bandwidth prediction
- **Advantage:** DWCP is general-purpose, not FL-specific

**4. CO2 (Communication-Computation Overlap)**
- **Similarity:** Asynchronous communication
- **Difference:** DWCP adds bounded staleness, vector clocks
- **Advantage:** DWCP provides consistency guarantees
