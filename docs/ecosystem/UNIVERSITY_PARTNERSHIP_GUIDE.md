**A:** Yes, semester-long pilots available.

### Q: What LMS do you integrate with?
**A:** Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, and custom.

**A:** No, credits are per-student accounts.

**A:** University retains ownership, non-exclusive license to NovaCron.
