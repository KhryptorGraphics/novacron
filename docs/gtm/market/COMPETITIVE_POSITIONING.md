### 8.2 Competitive Response Protocol

**Trigger:** VMware/Microsoft/Red Hat announces significant price reduction

**Response Timeline:**
- **Hour 1:** Assess impact (how many deals at risk)
- **Hour 4:** Convene response team (CRO, CMO, CFO, Product)
- **Day 1:** Analyze options (price match, bundle, ignore)
- **Day 2:** Execute response (communicate to sales, customers)

**Response Options:**
1. **Ignore** - If price drop still leaves us cheaper (e.g., VMware cuts 20% but still 50% more expensive than DWCP)
2. **Emphasize Value** - Focus on performance, quantum, Wasm (not just price)
3. **Bundle** - Add features or services to increase value without cutting price
4. **Match** - Price match for specific competitive deals (case-by-case)
5. **Offensive** - Increase marketing spend to highlight our advantages
